Data Deletion Instructions

OnlyFarms respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and all associated data.

Important: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including order history, messages, and reviews, will be permanently removed from our system.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (name, phone number, email, profile picture)
  • Your order history and transaction records
  • Your addresses and delivery information
  • Your product reviews and ratings
  • Your messages and conversations
  • Your pre-orders and subscriptions
  • Your Facebook/Google login association
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Data Retention Exceptions

For legal and business purposes, we may retain:

  • Transaction records (for tax and accounting purposes) - anonymized after 7 years
  • Fraud prevention data (to prevent abuse of our services)
  • Any data required by law to be retained

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Delete Through the App (Recommended)

1 Open the OnlyFarms app and log in to your account
2 Go to your Profile by tapping the profile icon
3 Tap "Settings" (gear icon)
4 Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
5 Read the warning and tap "Confirm Deletion"
6 Your account will be deleted immediately, and you will be logged out

Facebook Login Data

If you signed up using Facebook Login, deleting your OnlyFarms account will:

  • Remove all OnlyFarms data from our servers
  • Remove the connection between your Facebook account and OnlyFarms
  • NOT delete your Facebook account (you must do this separately on Facebook)

Confirmation

After your account is deleted:

  • You will receive a confirmation email (if email was provided)
  • You will no longer be able to log in to OnlyFarms
  • Your data will be permanently removed from our active systems within 30 days
